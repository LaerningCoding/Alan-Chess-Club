The OSC Chess Community

The OSC Chess Club is a community created for OSC students wanting to learn chess! Our goal as a community is to create a welcoming space where students can learn, practice, and challenge each other in the game of chess. Doesn't matter whether you’re a beginner or an experienced player, there’s a place for you here!
